Why It Matters

Used Car Scams Are More Common Than You Think

⚠️ The Risks of Buying Without Checking

  • Sellers roll back odometers to make high-mileage vehicles appear newer and more valuable
  • Heavily damaged vehicles are repainted and resold without disclosing accident history
  • Stolen vehicles are sold using fake or cloned titles — putting buyers at serious legal risk
  • Vehicles with outstanding loans can be repossessed even after you've paid for them
  • Flood-damaged cars look perfectly fine but hide engine and electrical problems that surface later
  • Open safety recalls may put you and your family at risk if left unresolved
